Cedar Planked Salmon

The smoky woodiness of this salmon is a perfect marriage of earth and ocean

Salmon and cedar go together like hot fudge on a sundae. Not only does the fish get imbued with the essence of cedar, but this technique is also easy, gentle, and health-conscious. We take this recipe to new heights by adding a tangy honey and grain mustard sauce, which is elevated further with a bit of rosemary and lemon. Is your mouth watering yet? Let's get started.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of grain mustard
  • 2 tablespoons mild honey
  • 1 teaspoon minced rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon grated lemon zest
  • 1 (2-pounds) salmon fillet with skin (1 1/2 inches thick)
  • Equipment: cedar grilling plank (about 15 by 6 inches)
